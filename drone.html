About

Pie-lot is a downloadable iOS app which allows anyone to build a drone using a raspberry pi with the additional requirements and download our App and connect to pilot your drone.

Requirements

- Raspberry pi3 with Camera add-on

- 4 brushless motors

- 4 ESC (Electronical speed control)

- 10 DOF IMU (GY-86 10DOF MS5611 HMC5883L MPU6050, 3-axis gyro + 3 axis accelerometer + 3-axis magnetic field + atmospheric pressure) GPS

- ADC for monitoring the battery
