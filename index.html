Chibb

July 2022-2020

Chibb is a 2D iOS arcade game that puts players in the role of a fearless balloon pilot in a world threatened by falling asteroids and comets. Inspired by the classic gameplay of Space Invaders, Chibb challenges players to maneuver their balloon across the screen, dodging falling obstacles. With colorful graphics, challenging levels, and intuitive touch controls, Chibb is the perfect game for casual gamers looking for a quick and entertaining mobile gaming experience. See preview

pie-lot

July 2022-2020

Pie-lot is a mobile app designed for iOS and Android to allow you to create a drone as an engineering project or as a fun hobby. Using my instructions you can control your drone using my App with the flexibility to customise your drone in the way that suits you. This project was inspired by my experience using the DJI Drone Controller, where I found it a great user-experience and thought it would be great to have for all or more specifically custom built drones.
