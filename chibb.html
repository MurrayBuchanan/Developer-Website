About

Chibb is a 2D iOS arcade game that puts players in the role of a fearless balloon pilot in a world threatened by falling asteroids and comets. Inspired by the classic gameplay of Space Invaders, Chibb challenges players to maneuver their balloon across the screen, dodging falling obstacles. With colorful graphics, challenging levels, and intuitive touch controls, Chibb is the perfect game for casual gamers looking for a quick and entertaining mobile gaming experience.

The process

Working on this mobile game alongside my studies has been a great learning experience. Chibb has allowed me to apply my current knowledge of Software Engineering to a real-world project. I've been able to improve my programming and design skills, and I've also learned a lot about project management. Beyond that, this project has really sparked my creativity and improved my problem-solving abilities, which I know will be useful in any career. Overall, it's not just about the game itself, but about building a strong foundation for my future in game development.
